More Player Profiles 🛡️

YRBK's searching mechanism allows for coaches to find more players that fit their niche. It pulls from any active user, no matter where in the world they are.

Better Messaging 🚀

YRBK allows college coaches to reach out to athletes directly, and have a secure and streamlined communication with recruits they find interesting with internal messaging capabilities.

Tailored Results 📈

The sorting and filtering capabilities in the player search feature allows coaches to find the "perfect" player to fit their teams needs.

More Information 🦉

With greater access to player statistics, GPA, SAT scores, teammate connections, and film all in one place, coaches are able to get a more comprehensive view of each athlete.

Stat-Keeping ✨

Simplistic, yet comprehensive tool allowing players to track and keep high school sports statistics. Bare-bones interface that allows anybody to get a hang of quickly and with ease.

Organization 🧰

YRBK allows for an automated organization of each coach's recruiting board and each players' college choices and suggestions.
